PULMONOLOGY

Welcome to the Department of Pulmonology

The Department of Pulmonology is one
of the most integral parts of Svasthya Healthcare.
The department aims to treat multiple
complex pulmonary challenges
that involve lungs and breathing.
The specialists provide some of
the most diverse and comprehensive
pulmonary, sleep and critical care for our patients.

Meet Our Pulmonologists

Dr Rahul Singh

Senior Consultant

Years of Experience: 13

Specializations: Pulmonary Medicine

A Senior Consultant in the Department of Pulmonary Medicine at Svasthya Healthcare, Dr Jayaraman S comes with 20 years of experience. His experience includes the diagnosis, treatment and care of patients with pulmonary conditions. He has expertise in critical care, bronchoscopy, thoracoscopy and the treatment of COPD, sleep disorder, tuberculosis, pneumonia, nicotine de-addiction, bronchitis, eosinophilia and so on.

Dr.Priya Sharma

Senior Consultant

Years of Experience: 11

Specializations:Interventional Pulmonology

A Consultant Intensivist and Interventional Pulmonologist, Dr.Priya Sharma is a well-known pulmonologist with nearly 15 years of experience. A graduate of Calcutta University and Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, his special interests and expertise include lung transplant, ECMO support for critical lung conditions, bronchoscopic & EBUS interventions using laser therapy and airway stenting. Other areas of interest are interventional medical thoracoscopy, sleep disorders, smoking cessation, COPD management and critical care medicine.
