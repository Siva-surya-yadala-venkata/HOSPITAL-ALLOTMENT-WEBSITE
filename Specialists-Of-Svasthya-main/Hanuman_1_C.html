Meet Our Cardiologists

Dr Kailash A Jain

Senior Consultant & Clinical Lead

Years of Experience: 13

Specializations:Cardiac Surgery

Dr Kailash A Jain is a Senior Consultant in the Department of Cardiology with nearly 40 years of experience in interventional cardiology. This alumnus of Stanley Medical College and Madras Medical College has special interest in and specialises in Percutaneous Coronary Intervention, Device Therapy and End Stage Heart Failure Management. He also has nearly 20 years of experience teaching DNB Cardiology Fellows.

Dr Ghayoor Ahmed

Consultant

Years of Experience: 11

Specializations:Cardiac Surgery

Dr Ghayoor Ahmed is an Associate Consultant in the Department of Cardiothoracic Surgery. The Kasturba Medical College alumnus specialises in adult cardiac surgery with a special interest in minimally invasive coronary and heart valve surgeries, and thoracic surgeries.
