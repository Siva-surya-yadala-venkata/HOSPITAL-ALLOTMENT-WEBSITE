DENTAL SURGERY

Welcome to the Department of MAXILLOFACIAL & DENTAL SURGERY

The Department of Maxillofacial and Dental Surgery
at Svasthya Healthcare focuses on
the diagnosis, treatment and prevention
of diseases and
conditions of the oral cavity
and related structures. It includes both
cosmetic dentistry and medical
dentistry. The department contains
trained orthodontists, oral and
maxillofacial surgeons, periodontists
(specialisation of gum disorders),
prosthodontists (specialisation of oral prostheses).

Meet Our Dentists

Dr.Vikram Patel

Senior Consultant

Years of Experience: 13

Specializations:Senior Dentist

Dr Vikram Patel is a highly skilled Dentist with 20 years of experience. He specializes in managing complex brain and spine tumours, including minimally invasive cranial surgeries and cerebrovascular surgeries. His expertise includes Skull Base Surgery (microscopic and endoscopic) for tumours like Meningioma, Schwannoma, Craniopharyngioma, Hypothalamic and Brain Stem Glioma, Cerebrovascular Surgery for Aneurysms, AVMs, Carotid Endarterectomy, Vascular Bypass, Complex Spinal Anomalies, and Tumours, as well as Minimally Invasive Cranial and Spinal Surgery.

Dr.Priya Sharma

Senior Consultant

Years of Experience: 11

Specializations:Interventional Dentist

Dr Priya Sharma is a Senior Consultant Dentist. He obtained his training in Dentist at the Sri Sathya Sai Institute for Higher Medical Sciences, Whitefield Bangalore. Following this he has trained in the UK and obtained the Fellowship of the Royal College of Surgeons in Edinburgh in dentistry. Subsequently he was a fellow in functional dentistry at the Kings College hospital in London and the Walton Centre for Dentistry and neurosurgery in Liverpool where he sub-specialised in deep brain stimulation, surgery for Chronic Pain, Spasticity and Epilepsy surgery.
