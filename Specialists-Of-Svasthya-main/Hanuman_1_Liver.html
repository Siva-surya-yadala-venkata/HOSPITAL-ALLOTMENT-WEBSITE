Meet Our Liver Transplanters

Dr Vikram Singh

Chair and Director

Years of Experience: 13

Specializations:Multi-Visceral and Abdominal

Dr Vikram Singh, M.D. is a renowned Multi-Organ Transplant Surgeon with a diverse clinical, translational, and academic background. He completed his residency in General Surgery in India and Urology in the UK, followed by a fellowship in Urologic Oncology with Mark Soloway at Jackson Memorial Hospital, University of Miami. He also completed an American Society of Transplant Surgeons (ASTS) accredited fellowship in Multi-Organ Transplantation at the University of Miami mentored by Dr Andreas Tzakis, Dr Gaetano Ciancio, and Dr George Burke. He has worked as a consultant transplant surgeon at the Oxford University Hospital for 12 years and as a professor of transplantation in the UK. Dr Anil Vaidya has made significant contributions to the development of Pancreatic, Intestinal, and Multi-Visceral Transplant Programs. He is also credited with pioneering the field of Transplant Oncology, which combines Multi-Visceral Transplant Techniques with adjuvant immunotherapy to treat locally advanced cancers in the abdominal cavity.
